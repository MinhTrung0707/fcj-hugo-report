* **Time Management and Enterprise Processes:** Need to further practice optimal Time Management skills to get accustomed to the pace of real-world projects, as well as strictly adhere to standard operating procedures of a large-scale enterprise environment.
* **Troubleshooting Mindset:** Although capable of resolving basic system errors, I need to improve the speed of Root Cause Analysis, especially for complex errors related to internal network connection flows or bottlenecks in the CI/CD Pipeline.

At the same time, practice confidence and agility when handling situations that arise in daily work communication.
